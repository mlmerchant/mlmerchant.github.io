Gobble Grid

4×4 tic-tac-toe with small, medium, and large pieces. Bigger pieces cover smaller pieces. No stack inspection.
Choose a reserve piece below, or click one of your top pieces to move it.
Rules: On your turn, place a reserve piece or move one of your own visible pieces. A piece may move to an empty square or cover a smaller visible piece. If moving your piece reveals an opponent 4-in-a-row, you lose immediately. If your move creates your own 4-in-a-row without first revealing an opponent win, you win. Refresh the page for a new game.
